Detailed Description

Irreps class.

Author
Sebastian Wouters sebas.nosp@m.tian.nosp@m.woute.nosp@m.rs@g.nosp@m.mail..nosp@m.com
Date
February 7, 2013

Class containing the irrep conventions and multiplication tables. The program requires Abelian point groups with real character tables, with hence .

Irrep conventions

The same conventions as in Psi4 (beta5) are used. For convenience, they are listed below:

Symmetry Conventions Irrep Number & Name
Group Number & Name 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
0: c1 A
1: ci Ag Au
2: c2 A B
3: cs A' A''
4: d2 A B1 B2 B3
5: c2v A1 A2 B1 B2
6: c2h Ag Bg Au Bu
7: d2h Ag B1g B2g B3g Au B1u B2u B3u
